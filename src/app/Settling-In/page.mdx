# Settling In

Find grocery stores, second-hand shops, and essentials for your new home. Get information on healthcare services and mental health support.

## Grocery Stores

### Rovaniemi

1. **Prisma Rovaniemi**  
   Teollisuustie 2, Rovaniemi 96320  
   Open Mon-Fri 8-23, Sat 8-23, Sun 9-23  
   [Prisma Rovaniemi](https://www.prisma.fi/myymalat/552112880)

2. **K-Supermarket**  
   A well-known chain offering a variety of groceries.  
   [K-Supermarket](https://www.k-ruoka.fi/k-supermarket)

3. **K-Market Hessuntori**  
   Located at Saaruantie 1, Rovaniemi, this market provides essential groceries.  
   [K-Market Hessuntori](https://lehtipiste.fi/product/lapin-kansa/)

4. **Rinteenkulma Shopping Center**  
   Features various shops, including grocery options. Open Mon-Fri 7-23, Sat 7-23, Sun 9-23.  
   [Rinteenkulma Shopping Center](https://www.visitrovaniemi.fi/shopping/shopping-centre-rinteenkulma/)

5. **Local Markets**  
   Rovaniemi has several local markets that provide fresh produce and local products. Explore options on platforms like Yelp for recommendations.  
   [Yelp Grocery Stores in Rovaniemi](https://www.yelp.com/search?find_desc=Grocery&find_loc=Rovaniemi)

### Kemi

1. **K-Market Kemi**  
   A local grocery store located at Kauppakatu 18, Kemi, offering a range of products.  
   [K-Market Kemi](https://lehtipiste.fi/product/lapin-kansa/)

2. **Shopping in Kemi**  
   Kemi also features shopping centers and local shops for groceries and essentials.  
   [Shopping in Kemi](https://www.discoveringfinland.com/finnish-lapland/kemi-tornio/shopping/)

## Second-Hand Shops

### Rovaniemi

1. **SPR Kontti**  
   Valtakatu 24, Rovaniemi  
   Offers a wide selection of second-hand goods, including clothing, furniture, and household items.  
   [SPR Kontti Rovaniemi](https://kontti.punainenristi.fi/myymala/rovaniemi)

2. **Kierrätyskeskus**  
   Teollisuustie 23, Rovaniemi  
   Provides affordable second-hand items and supports sustainability.  
   [Kierrätyskeskus Rovaniemi](https://www.kierratyskeskus.fi/myymala/rovaniemi)

3. **Fida**  
   Koskikeskus, Rovaniemi  
   A charity shop offering a variety of second-hand goods.  
   [Fida Rovaniemi](https://www.fida.info/myymala/rovaniemi/)

### Kemi

1. **Viikkotori**  
   Kauppakatu 9-11, Kemi  
   A flea market selling second-hand items and local crafts.  
   [Viikkotori Kemi](https://www.facebook.com/viikkotori/)

2. **Aseman Tori**  
   Rautatiekatu 9, Kemi  
   Another flea market in Kemi offering a range of second-hand goods.  
   [Aseman Tori Kemi](https://www.facebook.com/asemantori/)

3. **Goodwill Kemi**  
   Valtakatu 27-29, Kemi  
   A charity shop with a selection of affordable second-hand items.  
   [Goodwill Kemi](https://www.goodwill.fi/myymala/kemi/)
